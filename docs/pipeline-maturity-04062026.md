## How to read this scorecard

- **Op %** measures cadence reliability vs the workflow's expected cron firings over the last 7 days. <80% suggests the GHA cron is dropping and a cron-job.org pinger should be added (see `scripts/bootstrap_cronjob_org.py`).
- **Calibration columns** answer: of the rule_keys this agent's events feed into, how many have accumulated enough sample (n≥30) to be trusted? Tradeable adds the 'has edge' filter (PF>1, acc≥50%).
- **Adult** is the canonical maturity gate (acc≥90%, n≥30, PF>1.5) that unlocks BUY/SELL vocabulary in `thesis_agent`. Until anything reaches Adult, the system stays in paper-tier (WATCH/RESEARCH/AVOID_CHASE) regardless of how confident a signal looks. This is the CLEUF-loss discipline.
